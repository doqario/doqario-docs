## 🔄 The Problem: Documentation is Broken

Today's organizations suffer from:

- **Static documents** that quickly become outdated
- **Knowledge silos** between teams and departments
- **Compliance nightmares** during audits
- **Slow onboarding** due to scattered information
- **Implementation gaps** between specs and execution

Traditional documentation:
- Is written once, forgotten fast
- Doesn't validate, automate, or adapt
- Creates silos and requires translation to action
- Fails to maintain consistency across systems
